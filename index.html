About Me

Hello! I'm BETWEL KIPROTICH BETT

A creative and detail-oriented web designer and graphic designer with a passion for crafting visually stunning and highly functional digital experiences. I specialize in designing responsive websites, developing user-friendly interfaces, and creating compelling graphics that bring ideas to life.

I am also the founder of Elite Prestige, a luxury fitness brand, and Bett Enterprises, my entrepreneurial venture focused on innovative digital and business solutions. These projects have allowed me to combine creativity with strategy, developing both brand identity and practical business solutions.

My goal is to help businesses and individuals build a strong online presence and communicate their brand effectively, while continuously learning and tackling new challenges.
